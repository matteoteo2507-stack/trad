FTD Detector
Detects Follow-Through Day signals for market bottom confirmation using William O'Neil's
methodology. A significant index gain on higher volume, occurring on day 4 or later of an
attempted rally, is the signal that institutional buyers are stepping in. Requires FMP API.
IBD Distribution Day Monitor
Counts Distribution Days on QQQ and SPY. Risk classification: 0-3 days NORMAL, 4-5 days
CAUTION, 6+ days HIGH to SEVERE. Days expire after 25 sessions or if the index rallies 5%+
from the Distribution Day close. Requires FMP for daily QQQ/SPY OHLCV.
Macro Regime Detector
Detects structural macro regime transitions across a 1-2 year horizon using cross-asset ratio
analysis. Tracks stocks vs. bonds, growth vs. value, credit spreads, yield curve, commodity
momentum. Requires FMP or yfinance.
Market Environment Analysis
Comprehensive market environment analysis and reporting tool. Requires web search for
current data. Chart image optional for additional context. Gives you the full macro picture in one
output.
Market News Analyst
Analyzes recent market-moving news events and their impact on equity markets and
commodities. Requires web search. Use this daily to understand the narrative driving price
action.
Sector Analyst
Analyzes sector rotation patterns and market cycle positioning. Provide chart images of sector
ETF weekly charts. Get a rotation analysis: which sectors are leading, lagging, and at what
cycle phase. For crypto: substitute BTC, ETH, DeFi, L2, AI tokens for traditional sectors.
US Market Bubble Detector
Scores current market conditions for bubble risk using the Minsky/Kindleberger framework v2.1.
Tracks valuations, margin debt, IPO activity, volatility compression, and sentiment extremes.
Outputs a 0-100 risk score. User input required, no API.
SKILL AREA 2: Core Portfolio - Know What You Own
Portfolio Manager
Comprehensive live portfolio analysis via Alpaca MCP. Fetches actual holdings, calculates
allocation, computes risk metrics (beta, volatility, concentration), generates
HOLD/ADD/TRIM/SELL flags for each position. Alpaca API required.
Value Dividend Screener
Screens for dividend stocks combining value characteristics: P/E under 20, P/B under 2, yield
3%+, dividend + revenue + EPS all trending up over 3 years. FMP required. FINVIZ Elite
reduces execution time 70-80%.
Dividend Growth Pullback Screener
Finds high-quality dividend growth stocks (12%+ annual dividend growth, 1.5%+ yield) with RSI
at or below 40. Quality companies on oversold pullbacks. FMP required, FINVIZ recommended.
Kanchi Dividend SOP
Converts Kanchi-style dividend investing into a repeatable 5-step US-stock operating
procedure. Universe definition, quality filter, yield filter, entry trigger, invalidation conditions.
Knowledge workflow, no API needed.
Kanchi Dividend Review Monitor
Monitors dividend portfolios with 5 forced-review triggers (T1-T5). T1: dividend cut. T2: earnings
miss 20%+. T3: fundamental deterioration. T4: technical breakdown. T5: thesis invalidation.
Flags OK, WARN, or REVIEW. Local rule engine, no API.
Kanchi Dividend US Tax Accounting
US dividend tax and account-location workflow for Kanchi-style income portfolios. Aligns
holdings with qualified-dividend assumptions and account location (taxable vs. tax-advantaged).
Local calculation, no API.
SKILL AREA 3: Swing Opportunity - Find the Setups
Only use these when the Market Regime gives you a green light. Screening for setups in a
broken market finds great charts that immediately fail.
VCP Screener
Screens S&P 500 stocks for Mark Minervini's Volatility Contraction Pattern. Stage 2 uptrend +
progressive volatility contraction + volume decline into base + near pivot breakout point. FMP
required. Free tier sufficient for 35 stocks.
CANSLIM Screener
Scores stocks on William O'Neil's 7-factor CANSLIM methodology with multi-period Relative
Strength calculations. Phase 3.1 with 7 components. FMP required. Free tier sufficient for 35
stocks. FINVIZ web scraping handles institutional data.
Breakout Trade Planner
Generates Minervini-style breakout trade plans from VCP screener output. Pivot breakout price,
stop loss, position size, portfolio heat, Alpaca-compatible order templates (stop-limit for
pre-placement, limit bracket for post-confirmation). Local calculation, no API.
FINVIZ Screener
Converts natural language screening requests into FINVIZ screener URLs and opens them.
Free public screener works without an API. FINVIZ Elite auto-detected via environment variable
for faster results.
Theme Detector
Identifies which market themes and narratives are driving capital flows across sectors. Core
functionality uses FINVIZ public + yfinance (both free). FMP optional for ETF holdings data.
FINVIZ Elite recommended for stock lists. For crypto, this is essential: AI tokens, DePIN, RWA,
L2 rotation moves fast.

Edge Candidate Agent
Generates and prioritizes edge research tickets from End-Of-Day observations. Formalizes
market observations before you spend time backtesting them. Outputs pipeline-ready candidate
specs for the edge pipeline Phase 1. FMP optional, local YAML generation.
Edge Hint Extractor
Extracts edge hints from daily market observations and news reactions. Outputs canonical
hints.yaml for downstream concept synthesis and auto-detection. Local calculation only.
Edge Concept Synthesizer
Abstracts multiple edge detector outputs into reusable edge concepts with defined thesis,
invalidation signals, and strategy playbooks. Identifies when multiple observations are the same
underlying edge. Stops you from backtesting the same edge five different ways. Local
calculation.
Edge Strategy Designer
Converts abstract edge concepts into concrete strategy drafts with exact entry/exit logic, data
requirements, parameter set, and expected performance characteristics. Local calculation.
Edge Strategy Reviewer
Critically reviews strategy drafts before backtesting. Checks: edge plausibility, overfitting risk,
sample size adequacy, execution realism, survivorship bias. The quality gate that stops you
from trading strategies that only work in the backtest. Deterministic scoring on local YAML
drafts.
Edge Signal Aggregator
Aggregates and ranks signals from multiple edge-finding skills (edge-candidate-agent,
theme-detector, sector-analyst, institutional-flow-tracker) into a prioritized conviction dashboard
with weighted scoring and deduplication. Local calculation.
Edge Pipeline Orchestrator
Orchestrates the full edge research pipeline from candidate detection through strategy design,
review, revision, and export. The master controller sequencing all edge skills. Orchestrates local
edge skills via subprocess. Local calculation.
Stanley Druckenmiller Investment Advisor
Integrates 8 upstream skill outputs into a unified conviction score from 0 to 100. Inspired by
Druckenmiller's macro-to-micro framework. The 8 inputs: Market Breadth, Uptrend Analysis,
Market Top probability, Macro Regime, FTD Detector, VCP Screener, Theme Detector,
CANSLIM Screener. Output: one number telling you how aggressive or defensive to be. Local
calculation.
Strategy Pivot Designer
Detects backtest iteration stagnation and generates structurally different pivot proposals when
parameter tuning reaches a local optimum. Stops you from over-optimizing one approach when
you should be testing a fundamentally different one. Local calculation.

THE REAL EDGE
Every hour you spend gathering data manually, formatting spreadsheets, or running
repetitive calculations is an hour you are not spending on strategy and decision making.
That is what you should be optimizing for.

Data Quality Checker
Validates data quality in market analysis documents and blog articles before publication. Checks
five categories:
• Price scale inconsistencies (ETF vs. futures digit differences)
• Instrument notation consistency across the document
• Date and weekday mismatches (supports English and Japanese)
• Allocation total errors (section-limited checks)
• Unit mismatches
Advisory mode: flags issues as warnings for human review, exits cleanly even with findings.
Supports full-width Japanese characters, range notation (50-55%), and year inference for dates
without explicit year. No API required, works offline on local markdown files.
